Our History

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Adventures began with a simple passion for sharing the thrill of river running. What started as a small operation with just two rafts has grown into the premier rafting company in the region.

We've safely guided over 50,000 adventurers through some of the most spectacular river canyons, creating unforgettable memories along the way.
