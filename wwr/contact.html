Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring on my rafting trip?

We recommend bringing sunscreen, a change of clothes, water shoes, and a sense of adventure! We provide all necessary safety equipment.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refunds are available up to 48 hours before your scheduled trip. Within 48 hours, we offer rescheduling or a 50% refund.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 8 or more receive a 15% discount. Contact us for special arrangements for larger groups.
